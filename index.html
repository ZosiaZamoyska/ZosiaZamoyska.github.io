About Me

Student, undergraduate research intern and a woman in STEM.

I am a 2nd year, 3rd semester student at Korea Advanced Institute of Science and Technology, one of the leading universities in Asia. My majors are Computer Science and Industrial Design. Since July 2020, I am also an undergraduate research intern at Collaborative Distributed Systems & Networks Lab at KAIST, where I learn, research and turn my ideas into paper. I research concepts of urban city, placeness and social media. In my research I use deep learning and image preprocessing.

I have been passionate about Computer Science since childhood, so I can proudly say I am fluent in C++ and do not have any troubles learning new languages. I also am familiar with using python and java. As a designer, I am comfortable using graphic programs like Adobe Illustrator and have general aesthetic sense. Except for that, I am familiar with prototyping and using Arduino.

I am very communicative and active person that quickly adjusts to the environment they work in. My experience with design developed my creativity and taught me how to organise and utilise my brainstorm. As a result, I easily come up with interesting and useful ideas when working.
